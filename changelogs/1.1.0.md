## What's Changed

This is the first Release under the new OpenFoxes-Version of [Tiny2](https://github.com/cgevans/tiny2).

It contains a few refactorings and slight improvements:

- Formatting and basic refactorings by @Hirschfuchs in https://github.com/OpenFoxes/Tiny4Linux/pull/11
- General Refactorings by @Hirschfuchs in https://github.com/OpenFoxes/Tiny4Linux/pull/12
- UI Improvements by @Hirschfuchs in https://github.com/OpenFoxes/Tiny4Linux/pull/15
- Test-Pipeline & exact versioning by @Hirschfuchs in https://github.com/OpenFoxes/Tiny4Linux/pull/16
- #17: Added a release process by @Hirschfuchs in https://github.com/OpenFoxes/Tiny4Linux/pull/19
